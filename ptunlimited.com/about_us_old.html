About Us

James Macbeth, a native of New Zealand, graduated from New Zealandâ€™s Auckland University with a degree in Physiotherapy. He trained extensively in manual therapy under Robin McKenzie, Brian Mulligan, and other notable innovators. Macbeth practiced for 9 years in New Zealand and was the team physical therapist for high-ranking and national rugby teams before coming to the US in 1992.

Macbeth has been practicing in Warren Township since 1995, and is the owner & founder of Physical Therapy Unlimited. Macbeth specializes in manual therapy, vestibular therapy for the treatment of vertigo and dizziness, senior health, and orthopedics.

Physical Therapy Unlimited is a teaching site for Seton Hall University's Doctor of Physical Therapy program. In addition, Physical Therapy Unlimited is active in the Warren Township community. Please refer to our Events page for a listing of our current lectures, and fund-raising endeavors.
